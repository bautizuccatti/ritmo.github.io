we are RITMO: experience a new adventure

Welcome aboard the RITMO, where each trip is a unique and personalized experience. As a luxurious 46-foot catamaran, we are proud to offer exclusive charters designed to meet the wants and needs of our guests. At RITMO, we believe that the best moments are lived in the company of loved ones. Whether you are traveling with your family, friends or partner, our team is committed to providing a welcoming and memorable environment at sea. Our trips are much more than simple journeys; They are tailor-made adventures. From the moment you step aboard, our dedicated team will work with you to create the trip of your dreams. Do you want to explore hidden coves or immerse yourself in crystal clear waters? Do you prefer to relax in the sun or venture into exciting water activities? With RITMO, the choice is yours. But the RITMO experience is not limited to just the spectacular scenery and exciting adventures. Our talented chef will be at your disposal to delight your palate with exquisite personalized meals. From gourmet dishes to fresh snacks, each meal is prepared with attention to your individual tastes and preferences. Join us aboard the RITMO and discover a world of luxury, adventure and unparalleled hospitality. We are ready to make your next trip an unforgettable experience. Welcome to the RITMO family!
