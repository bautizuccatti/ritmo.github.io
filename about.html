CREW

Captain Simon, argentine water sports enthusiast and instructor, has 10 years of expertise and olympic training in kite-foil, windsurfing and sailing. He completed a sailing circumnavigation, accumulating 28,000 nm. His passions include free diving, fishing, and wakeboarding: great teacher and super host onboard, ensuring an unforgettable charter experience with all cautious and safe approach for cruising.

Chef/First Mate Lucia, also from Argentina, has sailed over 40,000 nm, including world sailing with her family and many crossings of the Indian and Atlantic Oceans. With qualifications in HR, Hotel management, wellness and nutrition, and RYA Day and Coastal Skipper and PADI Open Water certification, Lucia mixes her ocean enthusiasm with her cooking achieving a locally-sourced cuisine that reflects the regions she explores.

Simon and Lucia, the ideal crew for a nautical adventure, combining expertise in boats, water sports and deli cuisine. Perfect partners for a memorable and worry-free charter experience in the Caribbean!
