GRANADINES

The Grenadines, a paradise archipelago in the Caribbean, are known for their crystal-clear waters, white sand beaches and tranquil atmosphere. In this corner of the world, RITMO offers you the opportunity to embark on an unforgettable adventure. Travel to the Grenadines with RITMO is a unique experience, designed to immerse you in the natural beauty and vibrant culture of the region. From exploring remote islands to enjoying water activities like diving and snorkeling, every moment promises to be memorable. But it's not just about stunning landscapes and exciting adventures; Voyages aboard RITMO also offer a feast for the senses, especially when it comes to food. During your journey, you can delight in a variety of typical Grenadine dishes, such as fresh lampuki, chicken or lamb roti, taro leaf callaloo, fried plantains, fried conch and delicious goat curry. Each bite will take you on a culinary journey that perfectly complements the beauty of the surroundings. So get ready to taste the best of the Grenadines as you sail with RITMO towards the adventure of a lifetime.
